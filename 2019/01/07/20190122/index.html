Title: Fundamental groups of small covers

Abstract: Davis and Januszkiewicz [Duke Math.J. 62(1991), pp. 417-451] proved that the fundamental group of a small cover is isomorphic to the kernel of a map from the associated Coxeter group to $\mathbb{Z}_2^n$ , such description may not be suffcient in some specific problems. In this talk, I will describe this relation more explicitly based on the presentation of fundamental group we have calculated. Furthermore, our main result shows that the $\pi$-injectivity of a facial submanifold of a small cover only depends on the local combinatorial structure of the corresponding face. This is a joint work with Li Yu.
